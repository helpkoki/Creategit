Get to know about tekete

Tekete Management System is an automated customer care system that will allow customers to log a ticket relating to service issues. Our system is designed and developed to help you log and report computer incidents you experience with your personal computer. The system will help users within organisations manage and report incidents more easily and efficiently. Customers are able to register, login, retrieve passwords, capture service request and track these service request through a ‘single easy to use interface’.

The solution cover channels such as Web portal and Mobile App, calling service Desk, Email and WhatsApp chat board. Our system allows users to report incidents such as Forgotten passwords, Slow performance, Unrecognised usb, Network problem, Random shutdowns and others.
